Our Story

About Krishitha Creations

Krishitha Creations was born from a passion for making every woman feel beautiful in clothes that fit perfectly. What started as a small tailoring service has grown into a trusted brand known for precision, creativity, and personalized care.

We combine traditional Indian craftsmanship with modern design sensibility — and now, AI-powered styling assistance — to deliver outfits that make you feel confident and radiant.

Follow us on Instagram @_krishitha_creation for daily inspiration and behind-the-scenes content.

Transparency First

Our Policies

Privacy Policy

  • • Your name, phone, and measurements are used only to fulfill your order.
  • • We never share your personal data with third parties for marketing.
  • • Photos you share for reference are deleted after order completion.
  • • You can request data deletion anytime by messaging us on WhatsApp.
  • • This site uses no tracking cookies. Your privacy is respected.

Terms & Conditions

  • • Orders are confirmed only after discussion on WhatsApp/Instagram DM.
  • • 50% advance payment is required before stitching begins.
  • • Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary by ±3 days.
  • • Fabric quality depends on availability; alternatives will be discussed.
  • • AI suggestions are recommendations only — final choice is always yours.

Return & Alteration Policy

  • • Since each piece is custom-tailored, returns are not accepted.
  • • Free alteration is provided within 7 days of delivery if the fit isn't perfect.
  • • Alteration requests must be sent with photos via WhatsApp.
  • • Shipping charges for re-delivery after alteration are borne by the customer.
  • • Damages caused by improper washing are not covered.
